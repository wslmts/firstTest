Does the animation finish?
    If the numeric value is not an integer, the animation will end partway through
its last cycle. The animation will still run, but will cut off mid-iteration on the
final iteration. For example,  animation-iteration-count: 1.25 will iterate
through the animation 1.25 times, cutting off 25% through the second
iteration. If the value is  0.25 on an 8-second animation, the animation will
play about 25% of the way through, ending after 2 seconds.
